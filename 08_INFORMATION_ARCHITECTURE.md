## 🧠 1. COGNITIVE SPACES

### 🔷 A. The Dialogue Hub (Owner HUD)
Primary Purpose: The manufacturing plant for the Spirit. Where human intent and Brain telemetry reach consensus.
- **Consensus Center:** Dissonance Inbox (chat logs) and Pillar Architect.
- **Metabolism Pipeline:** HOLD triage list and Curation Studio.
- **Environmental Sync:** Live State Settings (Location, Rhythm, Mood).

### 🔷 B. The Neuro-Map (Public Proxy)
Primary Purpose: The agentic interface answering "How does this person think?"
- **Profile Tab:** 6-Layer Narrative Scroll (Identity → Intelligence → Scope → Proof → Output → Trajectory).
- **Systems Tab:** Complexity Clusters grouped relationally by problem type.
- **Thinking Tab:** Neuro-Web visualization and Strategic Sparks.

### 🔷 C. The Complexity Port (Evaluator Intake)
Primary Purpose: A Socratic intake funnel that forces evaluators to prove their problem's complexity.
- **Context Drop-Zone:** Minimalist text area for JDs.
- **The Simulated Audit:** Generated report mapping Spirit Pillars against fragmented systems.

- **Principle of Trajectory (Vector Mapping):** Sorted by Directional Vector, not chronology.
