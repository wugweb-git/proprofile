# 01. PRD & STRATEGY — IDENTITY PRISM ENGINE (IPE)
**Status:** Final Master Architecture (v6.0) — Ready for Engineering
**Core Mantra:** *Index the Logic, not the Title.*

---

## 🎯 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW & VALUE PROPOSITION
- **The Problem:** Traditional chronological resumes trigger the "Generalist Penalty," forcing multidimensional thinkers into flat timelines. Inbound inquiries are often low-complexity time-wasters.
- **The Solution:** A custom-built, mobile-first "Agentic Identity OS." It acts as a proactive digital proxy that indexes a user's *Decision Logic*, actively hunts outbound targets, and intelligently adapts its UI to inbound viewers.
- **The Narrative:** The system autonomously generates bespoke "Cross-Pollination" stories (e.g., how F&B logistics solve Fintech scale problems), serving them dynamically based on the viewer's specific intent.

---

## 🚀 2. GOALS & OBJECTIVES
- **Business Goals:**
  - Automate 100% of outbound job searching and portfolio updating.
  - Increase conversion rates for high-complexity (Tier-1) consulting or full-time opportunities.
- **User Goals:**
  - Present a unified "Specialist in Complexity" narrative without losing cross-industry nuance.
  - Protect the user's calendar from low-value networking via an autonomous AI gatekeeper.

---

## 👥 3. USER PERSONAS & ACCESS TIERS (RBAC)
- **Owner (The HUD):** Focuses on control. Manages pitch approvals, toggles MVS filters, and performs direct-on-page editing of raw Spirit Notes.
- **The Scout (The Match Brief):** Authorized via Magic Link. Sees the "Rubric Match," Cross-Pollination stories, and deep architectural logic.
- **Public (The Digital Handshake):** Mobile-first scrollers. Sees a high-level "Pulse," an Identity Anchor, and generalized proof of complexity.

---

## 🧩 4. CORE FEATURES
- **Feature 1: The "Hero State" & Offline Triggers:** Mobile-first entry UI with "Skill Clusters" and real-time physical/digital operational status.
- **Feature 2: The Hunter Protocol (Autonomous Outbound Engine):** 4-part background agent (Scout, Filter, Researcher, Pitcher) for autonomous hunting and auditing.
- **Feature 3: Daily Intelligence Briefing (Owner HUD):** Executive terminal dashboard synthesizing Hunter Protocol activities into actionable text blocks.
- **Feature 4: Intent-Based Availability Agent (The Gatekeeper):** AI chatbot replacing standard Calendly links, surfacing booking slots only for high-complexity problems.
- **Feature 5: Adaptive UX (Magic Links):** Middleware reading URL parameters (e.g., `?mode=founder`) to instantly rewrite UI vocabulary and data hierarchy.
- **Feature 6: Voice of Experience (Strategic Sparks):** Hybrid-gated 30-second audio snippets capturing raw insights from the field.
- **Feature 7: The Verification Layer (Trace Logs):** Immutable UI audit trail linking AI-synthesized claims back to human raw notes or code commits.

---

## 🚨 5. SYSTEM GUARDRAILS
- **Anti-Generalist Flag:** Strict negative constraint prohibiting the generation of the word "Generalist".
- **Auto-Reject (The Negative Dataset):** Filter agent archives any JD containing keywords associated with "Feature Factory," "Siloed Generalist," or "Middle-Management" roles.
- **Anti-Rework Philosophical Core:** The product's primary success state is the representation of "System Stabilization."
- **Consensus Success State:** A "Truthful Profile" is a state of consensus between the Human (intent) and the Brain (telemetry).

---

## 📈 6. SUCCESS METRICS (KPIs)
- **Zero Toil:** 0 hours per month spent manually writing or updating portfolio case studies.
- **Outbound Efficiency:** Hunter Agent identifies/audits/drafts pitches for >= 3 Tier-1 leads per week.
- **Inbound Quality:** 80% of calendar bookings generated by users with chat inputs >= 6.0 MVS.
- **Trust Conversion:** >= 30% click-through rate on Trace Logs and Strategic Sparks.
